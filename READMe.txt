Agri-Energy Connect ReadMe
Welcome and thank your for using Agri-Energy!

When running our program will automaticaly be directed to our login page, so make sure to register and login 
and become a member to enjoy the program to it's fullest capability.
Once Logged-in you will then be directed to our home page. you will notice a navigation tab above. Ensure to make use of it to quickly manuever aroung the application. 
On the navigation tab, you will be provided with options to choose from such as: Add Farmer, Add Product and Search
Depending on your user-type(Farmer or Employee) certain features will be restriced:
 => As a farmer you will only be able to Capture new products
 => As an Employee you will have full acces of the program with no restrictions

When using the farmer option you can create, view, update or delete your farmer details
When using the Employee option you can create, view, update or delete your Product details. You also be able to filter products according to their product type and production date
you can also search products by using the search option as well.
Once satisfied with the app you can logout at anytime.
